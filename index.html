Who We Are

AISL Services (AISL) is an integrated solutions company with a footprint in Uganda and Sudan (Darfur and Kordofan regions). AISL has been at the forefront in driving positive transformation among vulnerable communities through integrated solutions and collaborations.

Vision

To leverage our expertise in different parts to provide technical assistance.

Mission

To empower humanitarian organizations to manage their resources, reach their project sites, beneficiaries in the remote areas and to keep their assets safe and clean.

Our Services

Need Assessment and Registration

Comprehensive assessment and registration services for humanitarian initiatives.

Screening and Mapping

Detailed screening and mapping of communities and resources to identify needs and opportunities.

Capacity Building

AISL helps organizations enhance the capacity of their people, business processes and operational systems to achieve operational efficiency and peak performance.

Mentoring & Evaluation

Customized mentoring and evaluation services including:

  • Process monitoring
  • Aid distribution monitoring
  • Output monitoring
  • Outcome monitoring

Food Security and Vulnerability Assessment

Specialized assessment services to evaluate food security and vulnerability of communities.

Security Services

Guarding Services:

  • Guarding offices, guesthouses, and warehouses
  • Securing operational sites (food distribution centres, NFI distribution centres, shelter materials centres)
  • Using cameras, metal detectors and alarming equipment

Cleaning Services

Professional cleaning and maintenance services for premises and facilities.
